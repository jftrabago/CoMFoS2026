About

CoMFoS was initiated in 1995 under the auspices of the activity group "Continuum Mechanics Focusing on Singularities" of the Japan Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics. From April 2010, the activity group CoMFoS was renamed "Mathematical Aspects of Continuum Mechanics".

The last CoMFoS meeting took place on March 16–17, 2025, in Kanazawa, Ishikawa Prefecture, Japan, in conjunction with MNANP2025, a workshop dedicated to the Modeling and Numerical Analysis of Nonlinear Phenomena.
